How It Works

From entrance to checkout in 4 steps

The entire journey happens automatically on the shopper's own phone — no staff needed.

01

Shopper scans QR or opens link

Place QR codes at every entrance. Shoppers tap once, camera permission granted — navigation starts in 3 seconds. No app store. No sign-in.

02

AI identifies their location

DINOv2 reads the camera frame and recognises which part of the mall they're in — at 98.3% accuracy, faster than typing your name into a kiosk.

03

Shopper picks a destination

A searchable list of every store, restaurant, and facility. Select one — the A* algorithm instantly calculates the optimal walking route.

04

AR arrows guide them there

Direction arrows on the live camera feed rotate with the gyroscope as they walk. Route recalculates every 3.5 seconds if they stray.
