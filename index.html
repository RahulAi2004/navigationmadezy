⚙️ How It Works

From camera to arrow in under a second

Four intelligent steps happen automatically every time you lift your phone.

📸

Capture

Your phone camera captures a JPEG frame (0.7 quality, ≤2 MB) and sends it to the AI server over HTTPS.

🧠

Identify

DINOv2 ViT-B/14 generates a 768-dimensional embedding. FAISS finds the top-5 matches. Majority vote → your location.

🗺️

Route

A* algorithm computes the shortest path from your current node to your destination across the campus graph.

🎯

Guide

An AR arrow overlaid on your camera feed points toward the next waypoint. The mini-map updates. Walk and repeat.
